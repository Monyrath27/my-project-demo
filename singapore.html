Singapore

Experience a vibrant city with modern skyscrapers, cultural heritage, and lush gardens.

Places to Visit

  • Marina Bay Sands
  • Gardens by the Bay
  • Sentosa Island

Food

  • Chilli Crab
  • Hainanese Chicken Rice

Culture

Singapore is a multicultural city-state with Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Western influences.

Activities

  • Explore Chinatown & Little India
  • Night safari at Singapore Zoo

Rules & Etiquette

  • No chewing gum in public.
  • Follow traffic rules strictly.

Travel Tips

  • Use MRT for fast city travel.
  • Carry water (tropical climate is hot and humid).
