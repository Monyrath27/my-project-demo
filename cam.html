Cambodia

Explore Khmer heritage, stunning temples & scenic rivers

Places to Visit

  • Angkor Wat
  • Phnom Penh Royal Palace
  • Tonle Sap Lake

Food

  • Fish Amok
  • Nom Banh Chok

Culture

Cambodia is known for Khmer architecture, traditional dance, and Buddhist temples.

Activities

  • Temple tours at Angkor Wat
  • Boat tours on Tonle Sap

Rules & Etiquette

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples.
  • Show respect to monks and elders.

Travel Tips

  • Carry small change for tuk-tuk rides.
  • Stay hydrated (Cambodia is hot and humid).
